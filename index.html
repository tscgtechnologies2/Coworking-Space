Got Questions?

Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers to common questions about memberships, guest permissions, booking meeting rooms, and office amenities.

We offer a range of flexible packages to fit all working styles: Flexible Pass (hot desking in communal spaces), Pro Desk (your dedicated personal workspace with locked storage), and Private Offices (secure lockable configurations for teams). We also offer Virtual Office packages for digital mail handling.

Yes. All members receive monthly credits to reserve meeting rooms using our web app. If you require extra hours, they can be purchased at member rates. Non-members can also rent our meeting rooms on an hourly basis.

Yes. Day passes are available for individuals looking to access our open coworking areas and premium refreshments. Passes can be purchased online or at the reception desk during business hours (9:00 AM – 6:00 PM).

Absolutely. We hold structured weekly mixers, educational workshops, breakfast panels, and wellness sessions. These events are free for all members and designed to help you network, build relationships, and grow your professional skills.

Yes, members are welcome to host guests for meetings in booked conference rooms. For guest access in shared workspace common areas, day passes or guest registration is required to maintain safety and workspace comfort for everyone.

Yes. Our virtual office services include a premium corporate business address, digital parcel notifications, and secure mail forwarding packages. Virtual office members can also book meeting rooms at member rates.
